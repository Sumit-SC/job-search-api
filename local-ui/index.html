Step 1 refreshes from sources, Step 2 loads saved jobs, and Step 3 lets you run fast local filtering without re-scraping.

Step 1 - Refresh from sources

How it works: Fetches RSS feeds from job boards (or runs headless scrapers), then filters jobs by:
• Query: Matches job title/description (very lenient - "data analyst" matches most data-related jobs)
• Days: Only keeps jobs posted in the last N days
Results are saved to the JSON store for Step 2.
